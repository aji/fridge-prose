Fridge Prose

This is a silly little toy that turns the Top OpenSubtitles Sentences dataset into "fridge magnets". There's only 10,000 tiles to choose from, which sounds like more than it is!

Use the input at the bottom to search for tiles, then drag them into the area above to arrange them into your masterpiece. A grid for snapping can be toggled with G. Selected tiles can be deleted with Backspace or Delete. Drag while holding Shift to disable snapping.
